Attractions

  1. Badshahi Mosque, Lahore: Visit one of the largest and most magnificent mosques in the world, known for its stunning architecture and historical significance.
  2. Hunza Valley, Gilgit-Baltistan: Immerse yourself in the breathtaking beauty of Hunza Valley, surrounded by snow-capped peaks and lush green landscapes.
  3. Mohenjo-Daro, Sindh: Explore the ancient ruins of Mohenjo-Daro, an archaeological marvel and one of the world's earliest urban settlements.
  4. Naltar Valley, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa: Discover the serene Naltar Valley, famous for its colorful lakes, pine forests, and adventurous trekking trails.
  5. Shandur Polo Ground, Chitral: Experience the thrill of the highest polo ground in the world and witness the annual Shandur Polo Festival.
  6. Makran Coastal Highway, Balochistan: Drive along the scenic Makran Coastal Highway, offering stunning views of the Arabian Sea and coastal landscapes.
  7. Swat Valley, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa: Enjoy the lush green valley of Swat, often referred to as the "Switzerland of the East," with its picturesque scenery and pleasant weather.
